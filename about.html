About us

Driven by one purpose: positive impact across Africa.

We are strategic thinkers and problem solvers. What sets us apart is a commitment to understanding your unique needs, then delivering tailored solutions that drive real results.

Our mission

To grow from good to great in the eyes of our clients, employees, partners, shareholders, and the African public at large.

Our vision

To be the most valued and respected consultancy company in Africa.

No templates. No shortcuts.

Leadedge Consults Limited is a full-service consulting firm registered in Kenya and fully compliant with all legal and tax requirements. Established in 2011 as Leadedge Consults and incorporated as a limited company in 2024, the firm has grown from a business-development consultancy into a multidisciplinary practice delivering engineering and infrastructure, research, policy, ESG, capacity-building, and supply chain solutions across Africa.

We take on the challenges that matter most to our clients, and often, to the continent. Our performance is anchored in an experienced pool of consultants and a strong learning culture that together guarantee delivery on every assignment.

We invest continuously in proprietary models and tools that diagnose each client's specific requirements and address them effectively and efficiently. This is our competitive edge, and we refine it constantly to stay ahead of local and regional business environments.
